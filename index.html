How to play: All players open this page, enter the same room code, and hit Join/Create.
The first player to enter the code becomes the host and sees the Start button.
Goal: Get rid of all your cards. Last player holding cards = the DURAK (loser).
Attack: Select a card, click Attack.   Defend: Select a card that beats the attack, click Defend.   Can't defend? Click Take.
Hover over any card to see its trophic role and biome. Trump (bold border) beats everything else.
